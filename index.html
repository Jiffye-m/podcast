Product Vision

The Paralympic Truth is a powerful platform designed to raise awareness about the overlooked realities within Paralympic sports. Release 1 explores the root causes behind unethical decisions, asking the critical question: Why do they happen, and what can we do about it? Release 2 looks ahead, envisioning a future where fair play prevails and ethical choices become the standard of championing a movement that promotes integrity, transparency, and accountability in every aspect of Paralympic competition.
